Advanced Structural Steel for Tall Buildings & Critical Infrastructure

Business Vertical / High-Rise & Data Centres

BUILT FOR CRITICAL DEMANDS

India's rapid urbanisation and the surge in digital infrastructure investment are creating unprecedented demand for advanced structural steel solutions in two related but distinct building typologies — high-rise commercial and residential towers, and hyperscale data centre campuses. Both demand structural systems of exceptional precision, speed, and reliability.

MetalScope India brings its integrated engineering, fabrication, and erection capability to bear on these high-demand, high-complexity project types. Our modular building approach — where structural elements are designed as interlocking kits and factory-fabricated to tight tolerances — is particularly well-suited to both high-rise towers (where repetitive floor plates benefit from standardisation) and data centres (where phased capacity expansion requires maximum structural flexibility).

Key Capabilities

OUR CAPABILITIES

From concept to completion, MetalScope's structural expertise covers the full scope of high-rise and data centre projects.

Modular Steel Systems

Interlocked modular structural systems that enable factory precision, rapid site assembly, and straightforward future capacity expansion for both high-rise and data centre applications.

High-Rise Structural Framing

Multi-storey steel framing systems including moment frames, braced frames, and composite steel-concrete systems for towers up to 40 storeys and beyond.

Data Centre Infrastructure

Structural shells, raised floor systems, roof steel, and critical clearance management for hyperscale and colocation data centre campuses across India.

Composite Structures

Composite steel-concrete slab systems, steel-encased columns, and hybrid structural solutions that combine the best properties of steel and concrete for optimised floor-to-floor heights.
