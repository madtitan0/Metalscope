Who We Are

ENGINEERING THE FUTURE OF
STEEL INFRASTRUCTURE

Six pillars that define MetalScope's commitment to delivering excellence in every structure we build.

PILLAR 01

Engineering Legacy

MetalScope India carries forward decades of structural engineering expertise backed by the legacy of Royal Fab Group since 1984, transforming industrial steel construction through precision, innovation, and execution excellence.

PILLAR 02

Infrastructure at Scale

From industrial buildings and warehouses to bridges and large-scale infrastructure projects, MetalScope delivers integrated steel solutions designed for strength, efficiency, and long-term performance.

PILLAR 03

Built for Tomorrow

Driven by advanced engineering, certified quality systems, and modern manufacturing capabilities, MetalScope creates future-ready steel structures that meet evolving industrial and infrastructure demands.

PILLAR 04

Precision in Every Structure

Every project is powered by technical expertise, optimised engineering, and execution-focused processes that ensure durability, safety, and operational reliability across industries.

PILLAR 05

Trusted Across Industries

Recognised by leading organisations including RDSO, BHEL, NTPC, L&T, and MES, MetalScope has established itself as a trusted engineering and infrastructure partner for critical projects across India.

PILLAR 06

Strength Through Innovation

Through continuous innovation, research-driven development, and customer-focused execution, MetalScope continues to shape the future of steel infrastructure with scalable, sustainable solutions.

What We Do

ENGINEERING THE FUTURE OF
STEEL INFRASTRUCTURE

MetalScope delivers complete structural steel and infrastructure solutions designed for performance, precision, and long-term reliability.

End-to-End Execution

From concept and structural design to fabrication, logistics, and site erection, we manage the entire project lifecycle under one integrated system.

Built for Scale

We specialize in Pre-Engineered Buildings (PEB), steel structures, bridges, rail infrastructure, modular buildings, and heavy engineering solutions built for industrial and commercial demands.

Manufacturing Excellence

Powered by advanced facilities and engineering expertise, every structure is designed for strength, speed, efficiency, and operational durability.

Powering Industries

Our solutions support railways, warehousing, automotive, power & energy, logistics, oil & gas, semiconductors, transportation, and infrastructure development. As approved suppliers for ICF Chennai and Southern Railways, MetalScope delivers precision-engineered rail coach components.

Sustainable by Design

As a member of the Indian Green Building Council (IGBC), we are committed to responsible construction practices and future-ready infrastructure development.

Precision. Quality. Delivery.

Every project is driven by technical precision, optimized engineering, strict quality systems, and seamless execution that ensures reliable delivery from factory to final installation.

By combining engineering intelligence with execution flexibility, MetalScope delivers customized steel solutions aligned with timelines, budgets, operational goals, and global quality standards.
Chairman's Message

A WORD FROM OUR FOUNDER

Warm Welcome to Metal Scope (India) Private Limited.

Pre-engineered buildings outclass the traditional concept of construction. Metal Scope India Pvt. Ltd. focuses always on meeting the needs of the customers worldwide for durable, affordable, and versatile steel structures. Having been for more than three decades in the Steel Industry, we always keep the priorities of our customers in mind.

We craft high-capacity, efficient, cost-effective steel buildings, structures, rail cars & rolling stocks with no compromise in quality, safety, and on-schedule delivery — on time every time, benefiting from economies of scale.

Metal Scope, being the flagship company of Royal Fab Group, is now recognized as a "Total Steel Building Solutions Provider."

We aim to be an active contributor in nation-building by offering affordable steel homes, connecting the nation with steel bridges, and conserving natural resources for future generations.

Chairman — MetalScope India Pvt. Ltd.
