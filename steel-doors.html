STEEL DOORS

Engineered Steel Doors & Railway Structural Solutions

Business Vertical / Steel Doors

PRECISION ENGINEERED

MetalScope India has built an exceptional track record in the railway sector through the manufacture of precision-engineered structural solutions that meet the most demanding technical and quality standards in the industry. Our deep familiarity with Indian Railways' specification frameworks — including RDSO drawing packages, material standards, and inspection protocols — enables us to respond to complex requirements with confidence and speed.

RDSO and RITES certifications are central to our railway business, providing our clients with the assurance that every product MetalScope manufactures has been reviewed, approved, and validated by India's premier railway technical authorities. These certifications are maintained through regular surveillance audits and ongoing technical engagement with the railways' quality and safety teams.

MetalScope manufactures critical structural solutions for the railway sector from our Pondicherry facilities, where dedicated production lines, purpose-designed fixtures, and qualified welding procedures ensure repeatable, consistent output on every order. Our integrated quality management system provides full traceability from raw material procurement through final dispatch and delivery to the client's facility.
