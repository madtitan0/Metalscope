COOKIE POLICY

How MetalScope India uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your experience.

Last updated: 1 January 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They allow the site to remember your actions and preferences over time, so you don't have to re-enter information every visit.

2. How We Use Cookies

  • Ensure the website functions correctly and securely.
  • Remember your preferences and improve navigation.
  • Understand how visitors use the Site through anonymous analytics.
  • Measure the effectiveness of any marketing campaigns we run.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

TypeName / ProviderPurposeDuration
EssentialSession cookiesMaintain session state and enable form submissions.Session

4. Essential Cookies

Essential cookies are strictly necessary for the website to function and cannot be disabled. They store no personally identifiable information and are deleted at the end of your browsing session.

5. Analytics Cookies

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. All data collected is anonymised and aggregated. You can opt out via the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

6. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to view, delete, and block cookies. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality. For guidance, visit www.aboutcookies.org.

7. Third-Party Cookies

Some pages embed content from third parties (e.g., Google Maps, YouTube). These third parties may set their own cookies. MetalScope India has no control over third-party cookies — please review their privacy policies independently.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
