Railcar & Rolling Stock Manufacturing Solutions

Business Vertical / Railcar & Rolling Stock

BUILDING INDIA'S RAILWAYS

MetalScope India expanded into rolling stock manufacturing in 2019, responding to the Indian Railways' ambitious modernisation programme and the rapidly growing demand for high-quality coach components. This strategic entry was backed by significant capital investment in a dedicated, purpose-built manufacturing facility.

Our rolling stock facility spans over 350,000 square feet and is equipped with state-of-the-art fabrication, welding, and quality control infrastructure. The facility operates under internationally recognised certifications — EN/ISO 3834-2:2005 for welding quality requirements and EN/ISO 15085-2 for railway vehicle welding — ensuring that every component we produce meets the stringent technical standards demanded by Indian Railways and international rolling stock OEMs.

From sidewalls and roof structures to underframes and complete shell kit products, MetalScope manufactures the full spectrum of coach structural components. Our Cold Rolled Formed (CRF) railway products represent a particular area of excellence — we are among the market leaders in this specialist segment, combining high dimensional accuracy with consistent mechanical properties across large production runs.

What We Manufacture

PRODUCTS & COMPONENTS

MetalScope manufactures a comprehensive range of structural components for passenger and freight rolling stock.

Sidewalls

Precision-fabricated coach sidewall panels in stainless and mild steel, with high dimensional accuracy for seamless assembly.

Roof Structures

Structural roof assemblies with integrated stiffening members, designed for aerodynamic performance and long-term fatigue resistance.

Underframes

Heavy-duty underframe structures that form the structural backbone of the coach, carrying all primary loads to the bogies.

Shell Kit Products

Complete structural shell kits supplied as coordinated, matched sets for rapid assembly at coach assembly facilities.

CRF Products

Cold Rolled Formed railway components — our market-leading segment — combining precision forming with consistent mechanical properties across high-volume runs.

What Sets Us Apart

KEY STRENGTHS

Seven pillars that define MetalScope's position as a trusted rolling stock manufacturing partner for Indian Railways and global OEMs.

01

Dedicated Railcar Manufacturing Facility

A purpose-built 350,000+ sq.ft facility in Pondicherry designed exclusively for rolling stock manufacturing — with overhead cranes, dedicated welding bays, and end-of-line inspection areas.

02

International Welding Certifications

EN/ISO 3834-2:2005 and EN/ISO 15085-2 certifications ensure that welding quality across all rolling stock components meets the highest international standards demanded by railway operators worldwide.

03

Advanced Quality Assurance Systems

Integrated quality management systems with automated dimensional inspection, weld procedure qualification records, and full material traceability from mill certificate to finished component.

04

Expertise in Stainless & Mild Steel Fabrication

Deep fabrication capability in both stainless steel (for modern Vande Bharat and LHB coaches) and mild steel, using appropriate welding processes, fixtures, and distortion control techniques for each material.

05

Large-Scale Rolling Stock Production Capability

With 5,950+ total components delivered across all coach types, MetalScope has demonstrated the capacity to sustain large-volume, long-duration production programmes without compromise on quality or delivery.

06

Market Leadership in CRF Products

Cold Rolled Formed railway products represent a specialist capability that few Indian manufacturers possess. MetalScope's investment in precision roll-forming equipment and process expertise makes it a preferred supplier in this segment.

07

End-to-End Manufacturing Expertise

From raw material procurement and in-house tooling to final inspection and dispatch in purpose-designed transport fixtures, MetalScope manages the entire manufacturing value chain under one roof — ensuring accountability at every stage.
