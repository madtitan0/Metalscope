Business Vertical / EPC Projects

ONE CONTRACT. TOTAL DELIVERY.

Engineering, Procurement and Construction (EPC) contracting represents the most comprehensive form of project delivery — where a single contractor takes full accountability for all phases of a project from concept design through to mechanical completion and handover. For clients with complex projects and high programme certainty requirements, the EPC model eliminates interface risk and provides the benefit of a single point of responsibility.

MetalScope India has the in-house engineering capability, manufacturing infrastructure, and site execution experience to deliver structural steel projects on a turnkey EPC basis. Our integrated team — spanning design engineers, procurement specialists, production managers, quality controllers, and site erection crews — provides seamless coordination across every phase of the project lifecycle.

The EPC Model

THREE PILLARS OF EPC DELIVERY

MetalScope's EPC capability unifies engineering intelligence, procurement precision, and construction excellence under one coordinated programme.

Engineering

Structural design, 3D modelling, BIM coordination, load analysis, connection design, drawing production, and client technical approvals — all delivered in-house using international codes and standards.

Procurement

Strategic sourcing of structural steel from approved mills, hardware procurement, subcontractor management, import logistics, and supply chain coordination across the full bill of materials.

Construction

Factory fabrication under quality management systems, transport logistics across 28 states, and site erection by our own trained crews — with mechanical completion and structured handover to the client.
